Project Scope

Literature Survey

AI
Agile communication carries emotional and collaboration signals that are often invisible in traditional project management tools.

Signal focus: frustration + blockers (proxy)

Figure 1. Example trend of emotional risk signals over sprint weeks (illustrative)

Agile software development teams communicate through platforms such as Slack, GitHub, Jira, and video meetings. These interactions reveal more than technical progress: they also contain signals of frustration, burnout, demotivation, hesitation, expertise, and conflict.

Existing Agile systems focus mainly on tasks, velocity, and delivery status. They rarely connect team emotions with sprint performance, workload balance, expertise discovery, or inclusive participation. AgileSense AI combines NLP, affective computing, semantic embeddings, computer vision, and decision support to address this gap.

Research Gap

Following areas are the research gaps found in most Agile intelligence tools.

Emotion Awareness

Traditional Agile tools do not detect frustration, burnout, hesitation, or emotional overload before they affect team performance.

Expertise Discovery

Task assignment still relies on manual lookup or keyword matching instead of semantic expertise profiles and confidence-ranked recommendations.

Inclusive Communication

Most brainstorming platforms do not support hesitant contributors with private rephrasing, tone control, and participation balance analytics.

Research Problem & Solution

Proposed Problem

How can Agile teams detect emotional and delivery risks early, then act before productivity and morale decline?

Emotional signals in team chats, meetings, and task updates often go unnoticed. This can lead to inefficient sprint planning, delayed blockers, interpersonal conflict, uneven workload, and reduced team engagement.

Proposed Solution

AgileSense AI is a multi-module AI assistant that analyzes communication and project data, detects team emotions, recommends experts, predicts the impact of requirement changes, supports inclusive brainstorming, and provides actionable decision support to Scrum Masters and project teams.

Research Objectives

Agile Emotion Monitoring

Monitor emotions from text, meetings, micro-expressions, and voice; aggregate confidence and track how mood evolves across sprints and ceremonies.

★
★

AI-Based Expertise Recommendation

Map tasks to developers using Jira and Git history, semantic task embeddings, and ranked recommendations with confidence to improve fair allocation.

Requirement Change Impact and Sprint Replanning

Detect backlog changes, score risk across effort, schedule, quality, and productivity, and recommend data-driven replanning actions before the sprint ends.

★
★

Inclusive Brainstorming and Communication Empowerment

Support hesitant input through silent capture, rephrasing, tone control, and clustering, while visualizing participation balance for more inclusive decision making.
